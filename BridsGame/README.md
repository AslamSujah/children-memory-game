## ğŸš€ Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- No installation required!

### How to Play

1. **Open the Game**
   - Open `index.html` in your web browser
   - Or visit the live demo: [GitHub Pages Link]

2. **Choose Your Level**
   - Click **Easy** for 6 bird pairs (12 cards) - Perfect for beginners
   - Click **Medium** for 12 bird pairs (24 cards) - Moderate challenge
   - Click **Hard** for 20 bird pairs (40 cards) - Expert level

3. **Start Matching Birds**
   - Click any card to reveal the bird underneath the egg
   - Click a second card to find its matching bird
   - If both birds match, they stay open and you score points!
   - If they don't match, they flip back to eggs
   - Remember where each bird is located

4. **Win the Game**
   - Match all bird pairs to complete the level
   - See your score and star rating
   - Try again to beat your best score and become a bird expert!
